## Quickstart

This guide is for you to get started quickly with UMAP, and to select the combination of products that work best for your business. If you want to understand more about what UMAP is, do read about UMAP in the <a href="/payments/umap/overview">Overview</a> page.

<br/>

#### What can I do with UMAP?

UMAP caters to two specific categories of businesses—**Aggregators** and **Merchants**. Before you dive into UMAP product suite, explore which category you belong to.

<table>
    <tr>
        <th><Text as="h4" align="center">Aggregators</Text></th>
        <th><Text as="h4" align="center">Merchants</Text></th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>
            <Text>Aggregators are online/offline platforms that on-board multiple businesses and provide them value added services. Some examples include—</Text>
            <Text marginBottom="nano">
                1. A food delivery app that onboards multiple restaurants, and enabled delivery services for them.
            </Text>
            <Text marginBottom="nano">
                2. Website creation platform with a storefront, that plugin providers can integrate with.
            </Text>
            <Text marginBottom="nano">
                3. Insurance and loan marketplaces that can be used by any distributors to sell to customers.
            </Text>
        </td>
        <td>
            <Text>Merchants are online/offline businesses that provide goods and services directly to customers. Some examples include—</Text>
            <Text marginBottom="nano">
                1. Loan providers who enable customers to get credit in their account and enable collections for repayment.
            </Text>
            <Text marginBottom="nano">
                2. Direct-to-consumer brands that want to enable UPI as a payment option on their website/app or other messaging channels.
            </Text>
            <Text marginBottom="nano">
                3. Streaming platforms that provide online services for education or entertainment through subscriptions and one-time plans.
            </Text>
        </td>
    </tr>
</table>
