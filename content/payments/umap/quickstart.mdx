# Quickstart

This guide helps you to get started quickly with UMAP, what product/s combination works best for your business. If you want to understand more on UMAP, please read about UMAP in the Overview page.

## What can I do with UMAP?

UMAP caters for two specific types of users, `Aggregators` and `Merchants`. Before we dive into UMAP product suite, let's understand more on which user bucket you belong to.

### Aggregators

Aggregators are online and offline platforms that onboard multiple businesses and provide them value added services.

###### Examples

-   Food delivery app that onboards multiple restaurants and provides delivery riders for them.
-   Website creation platform that has a storefront that plugin providers can integrate.
-   Insurance and Loan marketplaces that can be used by any distributors to sell to customers.

### Merchants

Merchants are online and offline businesses that provide goods and services directly to customers.

###### Examples

-   Loan providers who enable customers to get credit into their account and enable collections for repayment.
-   Direct to Consumer Brands who want to enable UPI as a payment option on their website/app or messaging channels.
-   Streaming platforms who provide online services for education & entertainment through subscriptions & one time plans.
