<hr class="primary" />

UMAP empowers merchants with direct API access to issue refunds and contest disputes efficiently, leveraging the NPCI's UDIR framework. This system streamlines communication and resolution processes, ensuring merchants have control and visibility over transaction reversals and customer disputes.

This comprehensive approach ensures that merchants can efficiently manage refunds and disputes, offering a streamlined mechanism to resolve customer issues directly and maintain high levels of satisfaction.
