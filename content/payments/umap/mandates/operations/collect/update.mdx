## Collect Based Mandate Update

<img
  src="https://storage.googleapis.com/strapi-assets/latest/mandate-update-collect/mandate-update-collect.png"
  alt="Bridge"
/>

The merchant will have to call the **<i>modify Mandates API</i>** with the correct set of parameters. Post this, they will receive webhooks for two events: **<i>update operation confirmation</i>** and **<i>mandate status notification.</i>**
