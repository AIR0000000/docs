## Mandate Revoke

Revoking a UPI mandate ensures that no further action can be taken on the UPI mandate. This includes updating the mandate, sending pre-debit notifications for the mandate or executing the mandate. For revoking a mandate, there are two scenarios which need to be kept in mind -

- A merchant or aggregator can initiate a mandate revoke by calling the **<i>revoke mandates</i>** API. UMAP will send notifications to the merchant when **<i>the revoke operation is completed</i>** and **<i>the final status of the mandate</i>**. This is depicted in the below diagram -
