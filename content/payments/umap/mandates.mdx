# UPI Mandates

<hr class="primary"/>

### What is UPI Autopay ?

UPI Autopay is a recurring payments product built on the same rails of UPI which enables businesses to create subscriptions or one-time block based payments seamless via the user's favourite UPI app.  

### What can I do with UPI Autopay ?
* Enable limited stock purchases like IPO investments on your wealth app
* Create seamless subcsriptions for your services directly from UPI apps
* Reimagine Cash on Delivery and Try & Buy with single block multi debits

### UPI mandate statuses

A UPI autopay mandate will flow through the below statuses throughout its lifecycle -

<img
    src="https://storage.googleapis.com/strapi-assets/latest/Frame_109/Frame_109.png"
    alt="Bridge"
/>

`Mandate Initiated :` A mandate creation request has been initiated. 

`Mandate Live :` Customer authorization received & mandate is now active. 

`Mandate Complete :` Mandate executions are done, and now mandate is closed.  

`Mandate Rejected:` Due to error or no authorization, mandate could not be created.   

`Mandate Paused:` Customer has decided to temporarily stop executions on mandate.   

`Mandate Revoked:` Mandate was closed by customer or merchant before expiry.
