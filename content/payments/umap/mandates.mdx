# UPI Mandates

UPI Mandates is a recurring payments product built on the same rails of UPI which enables businesses to create subscriptions or one-time block based payments seamless via the user's favourite UPI app.  

## What can I do with UPI Mandates?
Choose one or more of our products under the UMAP Mandates stack to
* Enable limited stock purchases like IPO investments on your wealth app
* Create seamless subcsriptions for your services directly from UPI apps
* Reimagine **cash on delivery** or **try and buy** with single block multi debits

#### UPI mandate statuses

A mandate will flow through the below statuses throughout its lifecycle -

<img
    src="https://storage.googleapis.com/strapi-assets/latest/Frame_109/Frame_109.png"
    alt="Bridge"
/>

`mandate_initiated`&nbsp;A mandate creation request has been initiated. 

`mandate_live` Customer authorization received & mandate is now active. 

`mandate_complete`&nbsp;Mandate executions are done, and now mandate is closed.  

`mandate_rejected`&nbsp;Due to error or no authorization, mandate could not be created.   

`mandate_paused`&nbsp;Customer has decided to temporarily stop executions on mandate.   

`mandate_revoked`&nbsp;Mandate was closed by customer or merchant before expiry.
